Frequently Asked Questions

How do daily returns work?

Daily returns are automatically credited to your account balance every 24 hours after your investment starts. You can withdraw your returns anytime or reinvest them for compound growth.

What is the minimum investment amount?

Our starter plan begins at just Ksh 600, making it accessible for new investors to get started. We have various plans to suit different investment capacities.

Can I withdraw my investment before the duration ends?

Early withdrawals are subject to a 10% fee. Your principal amount minus the fee will be returned to your account balance. However, you can withdraw your daily earnings at any time without penalties.

How are the agriculture projects selected?

Our team of agriculture experts carefully selects sustainable projects with proven track records. We focus on projects that benefit local communities while providing consistent returns to our investors.
