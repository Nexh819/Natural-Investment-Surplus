Account Management

Everything you need to know about managing your Natural Surplus Investment account

How do I update my profile information?

To update your profile information:

  1. Navigate to the "Account Settings" from the dashboard
  2. Click on the "Profile Information" tab
  3. Update your information as needed
  4. Click "Save Changes" to confirm your updates

Your changes will be reflected immediately across the platform.

How do I change my password?

To change your password:

  1. Go to "Account Settings"
  2. Select the "Security" tab
  3. Enter your current password
  4. Create and confirm your new password
  5. Click "Save Changes"

For security reasons, you'll need to use a strong password that includes a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters.

How can I verify my account?

Account verification is completed in three steps:

  1. Provide your basic information (name, address, etc.)
  2. Upload a government-issued ID (passport, national ID, driver's license)
  3. Submit proof of address (utility bill, bank statement, etc.)
1 Basic Info
2 ID Verification
3 Address Proof

Each step must be approved before proceeding to the next. Verification typically takes 1-3 business days to complete.

Security

Important information to help keep your account and investments secure

How can I enable two-factor authentication?

Enhanced Security: Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of protection to your account. We strongly recommend enabling it.

To enable two-factor authentication:

  1. Go to Account Settings > Security
  2. Toggle on "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
  3. Choose your preferred method (Authentication App or SMS)
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete setup
Authentication App
  • More secure than SMS
  • Works without cellular service
  • Supported apps: Google Authenticator, Authy, Microsoft Authenticator
SMS Verification
  • Simple to set up
  • No need to install additional apps
  • Requires cellular service to receive codes

I can't log in to my account. What should I do?

Troubleshooting Steps
1

Verify your email address

Make sure you're using the correct email address associated with your account.

2

Reset your password

Try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.

3

Check your internet connection

Ensure you have a stable internet connection.

4

Clear browser cache and cookies

Sometimes stored data can cause login issues. Try clearing your browser data.

5

Try a different browser

Use a different browser or device to see if the issue persists.

If you still can't access your account after trying these steps, please contact our support team using the form below.
