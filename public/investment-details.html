Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum investment amount?

The minimum investment amount varies by plan, starting from as low as Ksh 5,000 for our Quick Starter plan. Each plan is designed to accommodate different investment capacities and goals.

How are returns calculated and paid?

Returns are calculated daily based on the percentage rate of your chosen plan. The returns are credited to your account every day and can be viewed in your dashboard. Your initial capital is returned at the end of the investment term.

Can I withdraw my investment before the end of the term?

Early withdrawals are subject to a 10% fee on the principal amount. However, you can withdraw your daily earnings at any time without any penalty.

Is my investment secure?

Yes, at Natural Surplus Investment, we prioritize the security of your investments. We have implemented robust security measures to protect your funds and personal information. Additionally, we are fully compliant with all relevant regulations.

Can I have multiple investments at once?

Yes, you can have multiple active investments across different plans simultaneously. This allows you to diversify your investment portfolio and maximize your returns.
